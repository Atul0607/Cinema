S U M M A R Y

Use of HTML

  • Images: Actors, actress, films
  • Hyperlinks: Different youtube videos and to shuffle between different pages
  • Multiple iframes are used
  • Tags: p,h,li,br,b,etc.....
  • div and span both are used
  • Table for ranking films in accordance with the gross revenue
  • Lists to prepare a list of actors and actress of different film industries

Use of CSS Properties

  • Color (both background and font)
  • Display
  • Text-align
  • Font-weight and font-family
  • Margin and padding
  • Border
  • Height and width
  • Position
  • Keyframes are also used

Use of JAVASCRIPT

  • Inbuilt Functions - setInterval and Date
  • Self made functions for different images
  • if-else statements for greetings at different time
  • Use of 'let' data type
  • Use of 'getElementById' and innerHTML
  • Array to maintain a record of actors and actress

Use of Animation

  • Mover animation is used that runs for 6 seconds linearly and infinite times as a loop

Use of JQuery

  • Effects: fadeOut,fadeIn,slideToggle,double click
  • Methods: click,mouseenter,hover
